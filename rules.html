How to Play Connect 4

Welcome to the Arena! The rules are simple, but mastering the game takes strategy.

Objective

Be the first player to connect 4 of your colored discs in a row (either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally).

Gameplay

  1. The game is played on a grid that is 7 columns wide and 6 rows high.
  2. Players take turns dropping one of their discs into any of the 7 columns.
  3. The disc will fall straight down, occupying the lowest available space within the column.
  4. The game ends when a player successfully connects 4 discs, or when the board is completely full (resulting in a tie).
