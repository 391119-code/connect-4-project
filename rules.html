Official Arena Rules

Welcome to the rules page! Before you step into the Arena or challenge the AI, make sure you know how the game is played.

The Objective

The goal of Connect 4 is simple: be the first player to form a continuous line of four of your own colored discs. This winning line can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.

Game Setup

  • The classic Connect 4 board consists of a grid with 7 columns and 6 rows.
  • There are two players. Each player is assigned a unique color (traditionally Red and Yellow).

Taking Turns

  • Players decide who goes first.
  • On your turn, you must choose one of the 7 columns and "drop" a single disc into it.
  • Because of gravity, the disc will fall straight down and occupy the lowest available empty space within that specific column.
  • Players alternate turns dropping one disc at a time. You cannot skip your turn!

Winning & Draws

  • Victory: The exact moment a player connects four discs in a row, the game immediately ends, and that player is declared the Arena Champion!
  • Draw: If the entire board fills up (all 42 slots are taken) and neither player has connected four discs, the game ends in a tie.
